Demonstration of Chemventure


Brief Intro

This is a chemistry education game under a Sci-Fi background based on Bitsy , designed by me, Jiahong Li. My design goal is an hour-long top-down adventure game, teaching players knowledge of a group of basic compounds and their making process.

Game Control: Use W, A, S, D or ↑, ←, ↓, → to control the avatar. Walk into NPCs to interact. SPACE bar to continue conversations.

Background Story

After the 50 years war, most knowledge of making chemical compound has been forgotten. People now are using the legacy technology to survive, but no one know how to remake them. Our civilization is dying. You are awaken in a secret underground facility. You had no memory of the past, but only one mission in mind. Gather the ancient secrets of making chemical compound and recover the civilization.
